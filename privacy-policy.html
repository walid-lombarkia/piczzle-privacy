Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Piczzle ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application Piczzle (the "App") on Android devices.

1. Information We Collect

Piczzle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device. No account creation is required, and no data is sent to any server.

The App requests access to the following device features:

  • Camera — used solely to take a photo that becomes your puzzle. Photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded, stored, or shared with anyone.
  • Photo Library / Gallery — used solely to let you pick an existing photo from your device as puzzle content. Images are processed locally and never uploaded or shared.

2. How We Use Your Information

We do not use your information for any purpose because we do not collect any. All photos you take or select remain on your device and are used only within the App to generate the puzzle.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties — because we do not collect any user data.

4. Third-Party Services

Piczzle does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. The App contains no ads.

5. Children's Privacy

Piczzle does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, the App is safe for all ages.

6. Data Security

Since Piczzle does not collect or transmit any data, there is no personal data at risk. All puzzle images remain local to your device and are cleared when you close the App.

7. Permissions Explained

  • CAMERA — Required only when you choose "Take Photo". The App asks for permission before accessing the camera. You can deny this permission and still use the gallery option.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES — Required only when you choose "Upload Image" from your gallery. Used read-only to display your image as a puzzle.
  • INTERNET — Required by the React Native framework for development tooling. The App itself makes no network requests.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have any concerns, please contact us at the address below.
